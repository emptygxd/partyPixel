Frequently Asked Questions

Still Have Questions?

We'll do our best below! And if you don't find what you're looking for, check out How to Play.

Party Pixel is the brainchild behind smash-hit party experiences like You Have No Clue, QuipCrush, BluffBuff, Doodle Duel, Trivia Terror, and more! Think of us as the rebellious offspring of old-school social staples like charades… the one who dropped out, then earned the family's respect by launching a boutique jam empire.

Maybe you first stumbled across us in a feature article, on someone's stream, during a cheeky ad break, or via a friend's enthusiastic recommendation. Whatever led you here, welcome aboard-our mission is to turn every hangout (near or far) into something hilariously weird, wildly fun, and utterly unforgettable.

At Party Pixel, we craft an eclectic lineup of experiences guaranteed to entertain everyone-whether they're gathered around a living-room TV, dialing in on Zoom, or tuning in via Twitch. From sketch-off showdowns and word-smithing challenges to lightning-fast quizzes and secret-identity mysteries, no player ever gets left out of the fun.

Since 2014, we've rolled out a fresh Pixel Party Bundle each year-five distinct games per bundle, each accommodating 1-8 participants. Have more than eight friends? (1) Well done on your instant celebrity status, and (2) many titles feature “audience play-along,” so every on-looker can join in the chaos.

We also offer standalone smash hits-like Doodle Duel 2-perfect for sparking uproarious fun on their own, any time, anywhere.

Everyone just needs any internet-enabled device-no downloads, no extra purchases. All it takes is a smartphone (or tablet/laptop), a web connection, and a clear view of the game screen. Now lean in and let the fun begin!

You've grabbed a Party Pixel Party Pack and rallied your crew-now it's time to jump in and play! Head over to our tutorial video and detailed walkthrough for everything you need to connect every participant. That guide also shows you how to unlock bonus settings and customize each game to your liking.

Just remember: everyone, whether in the same room or miles apart, needs a clear view of the screen playing the game (and to have the volume up!). That way they can spot the room code, join instantly, and stay in sync with all the on-screen action.

Need a hand? Swing by Party Pixel's Support hub—your one-stop shop for guides, tips, and troubleshooting walkthroughs. If you don't spot the answer, file a ticket and our crew will reply within 1-3 business days (holiday seasons might add a bit of wait).

Pro tip: if a player's device drops out mid-game, have them hit “refresh” on their browser—most of the time they'll pop right back in. And if that doesn't do the trick, just let us know—no one gets left behind!
